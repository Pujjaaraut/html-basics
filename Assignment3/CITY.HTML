Manjari is a rapidly developing suburb located near Pune, Maharashtra.

One of the key advantages of living in Manjari is its excellent connectivity. It is well-connected to Pune city via a network of roads and public transport, ensuring easy commutes for residents. The proposed Manjari railway station is expected to further enhance connectivity, making travel more convenient.Manjari is also rich in essential amenities. It has several reputed schools, healthcare facilities, and shopping centers within easy reach, ensuring that residents have access to everything they need. The presence of these amenities makes it a self-sufficient suburb where people can live comfortably without needing to travel far for basic necessities.
